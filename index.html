What is Air Max?

As the name would lead you to believe, all air max shoes contain a translucent plastic pouch in the midsole containing pressurized air.

While this technologically originated with the Air Max Tailwind, it would evenntually be made visible in the shoe, creating the cultural sneaker icon we know it to be today!
